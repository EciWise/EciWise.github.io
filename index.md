# ECIWise

<img src="/assets/logo.png" alt="ECIWise Logo" style="width:200px; margin: 24px auto; display:block;">

Institutional platform for academic support for Systems Engineering students at ECI.

---

## What is ECIWise

ECIWise is an institutional digital platform that centralizes and improves access for Systems Engineering students at ECI to academic support tools, integrating virtual and in-person tutoring, interactive study, gamification, communication, and artificial intelligence-based recommendations.

---

## Problem

- Monitor schedules are posted physically but there is no way to consult them digitally.
- Students must go to the classroom in person without knowing if there will be available spots.
- There is no virtual modality for tutoring sessions, everything is in-person.
- The satisfaction form is manual and does not generate useful statistics.
- There is no tutoring history or student progress tracking.
- Students cannot choose a monitor based on their reputation or specialty.
- There are no notifications for schedule changes or cancellations.
- There is no ecosystem that integrates study, communication, and tutoring in one place.
- There is no institutional tool where students can practice and prepare for exams with ECI-specific questions.

---

## General Objective

To develop an institutional digital platform that centralizes and improves access for Systems Engineering students at ECI to academic support tools, integrating virtual and in-person tutoring, interactive study, gamification, communication, and artificial intelligence-based recommendations, in order to strengthen learning, academic support, and reduce student dropout.
